            <p class="section-label">About</p>

            <h2>Painting, installation, and mixed media from Meanjin.</h2>

            <p>Shari O'Dwyer is a Meanjin/Brisbane-based visual artist working across painting, installation, and mixed media. Her practice draws on the textures and materials of everyday life — found objects, domestic goods, and vernacular imagery — assembled into environments that are at once familiar and strange.</p>

            <p>O'Dwyer's work has been shown widely across Queensland and beyond, including at the Rockhampton Museum of Art, Hervey Bay Regional Gallery, and numerous independent spaces in Brisbane. Her exhibitions often engage site, material accumulation, and the social life of objects.</p>
